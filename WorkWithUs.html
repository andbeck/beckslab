PDRA Position (2-years)

We are recruiting a 2-year Post-Doctoral Research Associate for a NERC funded project entitled: ‘Towards a general theory of ecological impacts of multiple, simultaneous stressors’. The project involves modelling complex population and community responses to multiple simultaneous forms of stress using state of the art bio-energetic food web and foraging models. The primary goal is to reveal how features of stressors and features of communities combine to reveal the predictability and the generality of multiple stressor effects.
